Prep Time:

20 min

Bake Time:

~40 min

Servings:

12

Ingredients


- 4 ripe bananas, cut into chunks
- 1 1/4 cups light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup butter, softened
- 1 egg
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup whole milk
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions


1) Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Butter a 9" x 5" loaf pan.

2) Place the bananas into a large plastic zipper bag, seal the bag, and smoosh the bananas with your fingers until very well mashed. Set the bananas aside.

3) Place the brown sugar and butter into a mixing bowl, and mix on medium speed with an electric mixer until light and fluffy, 1 to 2 minutes.

4) Cut a corner from the plastic bag, and squeeze the mashed bananas into the bowl of brown sugar mixture.

5) With the electric mixer on medium speed, beat in the bananas, egg, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and milk until the mixture is well combined.

6) Switch the mixer to low speed, and gradually beat in the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt just until the batter is smooth, about 1 minute.

7) Spread the batter into the prepared loaf pan.

8) Bake in the preheated oven until the bread is set and lightly browned on top, about 40 minutes. A toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf should come out clean.
