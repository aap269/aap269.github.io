Prep Time:

40 min

Bake Time:

20 min

Servings:

6

Ingredients


CAKE
- 3/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup buttermilk
- 1/4 cup butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/3 cup finely chopped pecans, toasted


GLAZED PECANS
-- 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
-- 1/3 cup pecan halves


FROSTING
- 3 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 3 tablespoons butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon maple syrup
- 1 1/2 cups confectioners sugar

Directions


CAKE
1) Preheat oven to 350°.
2) In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugars, baking soda and salt.
3) In a separate bowl, combine the buttermilk, butter, syrup and vanilla.
4) Stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients just until combined.
5) Fold in chopped pecans.
6) Pour into two 6-in. round baking pans coated with cooking spray.
7) Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks to cool completely.


GLAZED PECANS
1) In a small saucepan, bring corn syrup to a boil.
2) Cook and stir for 1 minute or until slightly thickened.
3) Remove from heat; stir in pecan halves until coated.
4) Place in a single layer on a foil-lined baking sheet.
5) Bake at 425° for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown. Cool.

FROSTING
1) In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, butter and syrup until smooth.
2) Beat in confectioners' sugar.
3) Place one cake layer on a serving plate; spread with 1/2 cup frosting.
4) Top with second layer; frost top and sides of cake.
5) Garnish with glazed pecans. Store in the refrigerator.
