Prep Time:

1.5 hours

Bake Time:

22 min

Servings:

12-14

Ingredients


CAKE
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 cups white chocolate chips
- 7 large marshmallows, cut in half crosswise
- Cinnamon or cocoa, for dusting.
- 14 chocolate-covered pretzels (optional)

Directions


1) Preheat oven to 350Â°.
2) Using a stand-up mixer or hand mixer, cream the butter and sugar.
3) Add vanilla and eggs and beat, one at a time.
4) Melt 1 cup of white chocolate chips by microwaving them at 30-second intervals and stirring in between. Fold into batter.
5) Add the rest of the dry ingredients and the milk and stir to combine.
6) Fill cupcake cups about 2/3 full.
7) Bake for 18 - 22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool completely.
8) Melt the second cup of white chocolate chips and place in a shallow bowl.
9) Dip the top of each cupcake into the melted chocolate, placing each coated cupcake on a rack to cool.
10) Set oven to broil. Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper and arrange the marshmallows, cut side down. Broil until lightly browned. With a metal spatula, place a toasted marshmallow on top of each cupcake.
11) Dust each marshmallow with a pinch of cinnamon or cocoa powder.
12) Stick each pretzel into the side of each cupcake about halfway through to represent a handle on a mug of delicious hot chocolate.
