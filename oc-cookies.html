Prep Time:

20 min

Bake Time:

15 min

Servings:

48

Ingredients


- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 cup butter, melted (1½ sticks)
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 1/2 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 1/2 cups old-fashioned oats
- 1 cup dried cranberries

Directions


1) Preheat oven to 325°.
2) Melt butter and let cool slightly.
3) Mix flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.
4) In a medium bowl, cream together the melted butter, brown sugar and white sugar until well blended.
5) Beat in the cinnamon, vanilla and egg for 2-3 minutes until light and creamy.
6) Mix in the flour mixture until just blended. Add the oats and cranberries. Mix until just blended.
7) Place 1/4 cup balls of dough onto parchment lined cookie sheet and bake for 14-16 minutes.
