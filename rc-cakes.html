Prep Time:

30 min

Bake Time:

~30 min

Servings:

14

Ingredients


CAKE
- 3/4 cups flour
- 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- 2 large eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup buttermilk


FROSTING
3/4 cup butter
1/2 cup raspberry preserves
3 tablespoons heavy cream
1/4 teaspoon salt
3-4 cups powdered sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions



CAKE

1) Preheat oven to 350Â°.
2) In a large bowl, whisk the cocoa powder, flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together until thoroughly combined. Set aside.
3) In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar, brown sugar, oil, and vanilla together until completely smooth.
4) Pour half of the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients. Then half of the milk. Gently stir - about 5 stirs. Repeat with the remaining wet ingredients and milk. Stir until just combined; do not overmix. The batter won't be very thick.
5) Pour/spoon the batter into a 10" x 13" pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool completely before frosting.


FROSTING
1) Beat softened butter on medium speed for about 3-4 minutes until completely smooth and creamy.
2) Add the confectioners' sugar, cream, vanilla extract, and salt with the mixer running. Increase to high speed and beat for 1 minute.
3) Add the raspberry preserves and beat until thick and creamy, about 2 full minutes. Add more confectioners' sugar if frosting is too thin, is separating (due to the fruity jam) or looks too wet. And then add more salt to offset added sweetness.
