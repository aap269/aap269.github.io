Prep Time:

1.5 hours

Bake Time:

12 min

Servings:

16

Ingredients


- 1 chocolate cake mix
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup of milk
- 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
- 1 jar of marshmallow fluff
- 1 canister of vanilla frosting
- 1 teaspoon vanilla exgtract

Directions


1) Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

2) In a mixer, combine cake mix, eggs, water and oil. Whip together for 2-3 minutes.

3) On a parchment paper or Silpat lined baking sheet, scoop out batter into round mounds.

4) Bake for 10-12 minutes. It is better to overbake than underbake. Cool.

5) For the icing, mix the frosting and fluff in a large bowl with the vanilla extract.

6) To fill, pipe icing onto the bottom cookie (wrong side up), then top with another cookie (right side up).
